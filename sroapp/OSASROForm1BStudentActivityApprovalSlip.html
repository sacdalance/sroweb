REMINDERS

  1. This Approval Slip certifies that the activity proposed by the student organization listed above has been reviewed and approved by the Organization Adviser, the Student Relations Officer, and the Director of Student Affairs.
  2. If the activity will take place on-campus, the organization must submit a copy of this slip to the UPB Security Office. The organization may submit either this printed copy (retaining a scanned version for their records) or a photocopy. Additionally, if visitors (i.e., individuals who are not current students or employees of UPB) are expected to attend, the organization must attach a list of the invited individuals and/or organizations/institutions.
  3. The organization must obtain and submit approved copies of all necessary forms to the relevant offices. These may include CPDMO Forms (required for using university equipment) and Job Order Forms (required for employing university staff).
  4. If there are any changes to the event’s date or venue, the organization must inform and coordinate with the Student Relations Officer.
  5. Tampering with this document in any way is strictly prohibited. Security measures are in place to verify the authenticity of this document and confirm that the details match those in the SRO system. Any violation may result in disciplinary action, including but not limited to suspension or revocation of the organization's recognized status.
